About Us

MY MOTHER TRUST

MY MOTHER TRUST is a registered non-governmental organization (NGO) established in 2021 in Thiruvarur, Tamil Nadu. Our primary mission is to offer hope and healing to individuals suffering from chronic and viral diseases, specifically cancer, AIDS, and other communicable viral infections.

In addition to health care, we actively promote medical camps, environment drives, and career guidance. We are committed to fostering women's empowerment through vocational training and offering educational materials to children in rural government schools.
